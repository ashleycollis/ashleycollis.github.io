My Story

Hi, I'm Ashley! I am an energetic software developer with a passion for coding applications that serve consumer needs. I have built fullstack web and mobile applications using Javascript along with relevant libraries.

My career goal has always been to pursue challenging and enjoyable work that improves the livelihood of as many people as possible. Before software development, I worked in marketing and legal services for a few years following my graduation from Cornell University. I was initially set on attending law school but shifted paths after discovering my love for software development. Programming is a fun way for me to make meaningful contributions to society on a large scale. I love expanding my skills and pushing to solve novel problems while building socially beneficial technology.
